Craps Rules

The rules for this game of craps is you win on even doubles and lose on a 7 or 11.

In a real game of Craps, the ruleset used here would be a simplified version of hard betting in Craps. Generally, bets are made on whether or not a 7 or 11 would roll. There are further bets on if a 7 or 11 would be rolled before or after a non-7/11 roll is made. For clarification of these rules and further detail, please refer to the following link:
